GRILLED CHEEZ - THE GOURMET WAY

Wanna try the best grilled cheese you'll ever have?

Follow this one simple recipe to get a taste of heaven!

Don't forget to like, comment and subscribe!

Ingredients:

Remember that magic is in the ingredients!

  • Bread
  • Cheese!
  • Eggz

Pro tip! Try using mozzarella instead of cheddar!

Cooking steps:

  1. Get two slices of bread and put yo cheez between em.
  2. Smash the sides of your bread together so that they become one. Just like lovers.
  3. Cover your bread in egg that you have previously beat.
  4. Grill the shit out of that goodness!
  5. Serve stringy :))
